## Category A: KYC Required (Identity Verification)

Providers in this category typically require identity verification during signup, especially for business or high-volume plans. They often target enterprise customers and have compliance procedures.

### 1. Bright Data (formerly Luminati)

- **Pricing:** Starting from $3.50/GB (monthly plans), pay-as-you-go $4.00/GB. 7-day free trial available.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: ~150 million residential IPs
  - Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS5 (with UDP)
  - Geo-targeting: Country, city, ASN, ZIP code, coordinates, OS
  - Rotation: Per request, customizable sticky sessions via Proxy Manager
  - Extras: API, browser extension, Proxy Manager, dedicated IPs, IPv6-only IPs
  - Support: 24/7 via live chat, tickets, dedicated account manager
- **US IP Availability:** Excellent – city, ZIP, and ASN-level targeting across the US.
- **KYC Note:** Explicitly known to require KYC verification for business accounts; the process can be extensive for large-scale usage.

### 2. Oxylabs

- **Pricing:** Starting from $3.49/GB (monthly), pay-as-you-go $4/GB. Discount codes often available (e.g., 50% off).
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: Large, Tier-1 IP quality
  - Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS5
  - Geo-targeting: Country, city, state-level
  - Rotation: Per request; session control up to 30 minutes
  - Focus on large-scale data collection with high uptime
- **US IP Availability:** Strong – city and state-level US targeting supported.
- **KYC Note:** Enterprise-oriented; requires identity and business verification for signup.

### 3. SOAX

- **Pricing:** Starting from $4.00/GB. Volume discounts reduce to ~$2.46/GB for 1TB. 3-day trial for $1.99.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: 155 million (advertised)
  - Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS5 (UDP/QUIC)
  - Geo-targeting: Country, state, city, ASN, IP uniqueness, stability filters
  - Rotation: Every request, up to 60 minutes, custom sessions
  - Support: 24/7 live chat
  - Extras: Reporting API, customer success manager
- **US IP Availability:** Very good – city and ASN targeting available for US.
- **KYC Note:** Business-focused provider; standard identity verification required.

### 4. NetNut

- **Pricing:** Starting from $99 for 28 GB ($3.53/GB). 7-day free trial for businesses.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: 85 million (advertised)
  - Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS5
  - Geo-targeting: Country, city, state, ASN
  - Rotation: Per request
  - Detailed usage statistics and analytics
- **US IP Availability:** Excellent – strong coverage in US and UK with city/state targeting.
- **KYC Note:** Targets medium and large businesses; requires company and identity verification.

### 5. DataImpulse

- **Pricing:** Starting from $1.00/GB (5 GB for $5). Non-expiring traffic, 5 GB trial for $5.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: 90 million
  - Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS5 (with UDP)
  - Geo-targeting: Countries, states, cities, ZIP codes, ASNs
  - Rotation: Every request, sessions up to 120 minutes
  - Support: 24/7 via live chat, email
  - Extras: API, customer success manager for premium plans
- **US IP Availability:** Yes – detailed US targeting including ZIP and ASN.
- **KYC Note:** While affordable, DataImpulse requires standard business verification for paid accounts.

### 6. Infatica

- **Pricing:** Starting from $3.84/GB. Pay-as-you-go $4.00/GB. 7-day trial for $4.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: 15+ million monthly IPs
  - Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS5
  - Geo-targeting: Country, state, city, ASN, ZIP
  - Rotation: Every request, 5–60 minute intervals, manual rotation
  - Support: 24/7 via tickets, chat, email
  - Unlimited threads
- **US IP Availability:** Yes – includes city and ZIP targeting in the US.
- **KYC Note:** Enterprise service; identity and business verification required.

### 7. IPRoyal

- **Pricing:** Starting from $12 for 2 GB ($6.00/GB). Pay-as-you-go $6.25/GB.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: 32+ million
  - Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS5
  - Geo-targeting: Country, state, city
  - Rotation: Every request, customizable sticky up to 24 hours
  - Support: 24/7 live chat
  - Extras: Proxy tester, browser extension, documentation
- **US IP Availability:** Yes – covers US with city-level targeting.

### 8. Rayobyte (formerly Blazing SEO)

- **Pricing:** Starting from $3.50/GB. 2-day free trial with 50 MB. Non-expiring packages.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: 36 million

  - Rotation: Every request, sessions 1–120 minutes

  - Extras: Browser extension

- **KYC Note:** Business-oriented provider; requires standard KYC for paid accounts.

## Category B: No KYC Required (Anonymous)

Providers in this category allow users to sign up and start using proxies without formal identity verification. They often have simpler, self-service models and minimal onboarding.

### 1. Smartproxy (now Decodo)

- **Pricing:** Starting from $2.00/GB for 2 GB monthly plan. Pay-as-you-go $8.50/GB. 3-day trial with 100 MB.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: Large rotating residential network
  - Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, SOCKS5
  - Geo-targeting: Country, city, US states, ZIP code (US)
  - Rotation: Sticky sessions (24 hours) or per-request rotation
  - User-friendly dashboard and integration tools (anti-detect browser)
  - Granular scaling – small plans available
- **US IP Availability:** Excellent – ZIP-level targeting in the US, plus city/state.
- **KYC Note:** No formal KYC for starter plans; signup requires only email and payment. Appears in search results for "residential proxy no KYC verification".

### 2. Webshare

- **Pricing:** Starting from $1.75/GB (1 GB plan). Discounts for larger volumes (down to ~$1.13/GB for 500 GB). 50% off first purchase.
- **Key Features:**
  - Pool: 80 million
  - Protocols: HTTP, SOCKS5
  - Geo-targeting: Country-level only
  - Rotation: Every request, customizable sticky sessions (5 minutes)
  - Extremely simple UI, good for beginners
  - API and basic documentation
- **US IP Availability:** Yes – 195 countries including full US coverage.
- **KYC Note:** No identity verification required; quick signup with email and payment. Associated with "no KYC" searches and known for accessibility.
